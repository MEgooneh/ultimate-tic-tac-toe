How to play

The board is a 3x3 grid of smaller 3x3 boards. Win three small boards in a row to win the game.

  1. X goes first and can play in any cell on any small board.
  2. Your move sends your opponent to the small board matching the cell position you picked. For example, playing in the top-right cell sends them to the top-right board.
  3. If you're sent to a board that's already won or full, you can play anywhere.
  4. Win a small board by getting three in a row, just like regular tic-tac-toe.
  5. Win three small boards in a row on the big grid to win the game.
